State Standards Alignment

Understanding AI: A Conceptual Approach
Chapter 3, Lesson 3.1: The New Communication Landscape

Lesson 3.1 introduces students to prompt engineering through the professional model of developmental editor and ghost writer. Students learn that effective AI communication is a learnable skill involving iteration, editorial judgment, and clear direction. This lesson aligns with multiple state and national standards frameworks through communication practices, professional skill development, and technology literacy.
